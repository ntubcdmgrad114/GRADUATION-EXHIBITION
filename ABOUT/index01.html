BEYOND BOUNDARIES 邊界以外

「邊界以外」象徵著學生們不斷突破既有框架，展現自身價值與潛力。 如同本系以創新設計思維創造商業價值，學生們的創作涵蓋多元領域， 藉由突破各種有形無形的界線，到達邊界以外的理想狀態。 每件作品不僅是設計技巧的展現， 更是學生勇敢突破障礙、開創新局的證明。
"Beyond Boundaries" represents the continuous effort of students to break through existing frameworks and showcase their unique value and potential. Just as our department generates commercial value through innovative design thinking, students' creations span across various fields. By transcending both visible and invisible boundaries, we aim to reach a state beyond limitations. Each work is not only a demonstration of design skills, but also a testament to the students' courage to overcome obstacles and create new possibilities.
